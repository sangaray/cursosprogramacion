## Tricks

1. If you want to stop the ai inline complition:
   - Press tap just at the end of the word you wrote without press space
   - Press ctrl + right arrow to acept word by word the suggestion
   - Press ctrl + space to ask the editor to reopen the emmeth list
   - Press esc to stop and erase the ai suggestion
